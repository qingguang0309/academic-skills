# 顶刊句式库（材料/化学）

按 section 组织的可复用句式框架。改写时优先套用这些模式，而不是逐字直译作者原意——
换句式往往比换单词更能提升"顶刊感"。方括号 `[...]` 是待填的具体内容；这些是通用科学句框，
不是从任何特定来源摘抄，用时按语境改造。

**用法**：先判断这句话在段落里承担什么功能（建立重要性 / 指出 gap / 报告结果 / 比较 / 归因 / hedge），
再从对应小节取一个框架填入作者的具体内容。不要机械套同一个框架——同一功能给了多个变体就是让你换着用。

## Introduction：建立重要性

避免"能源危机/环境污染"式大帽子开场；从本领域的具体技术问题切入。

- `[Material/process] has emerged as a promising route to [specific goal], owing to its [specific property].`
- `The [performance metric] of [system] is largely governed by [factor], which remains difficult to control.`
- `Achieving [target] requires [requirement], yet [the intrinsic limitation].`

## Introduction：指出 gap

Gap 要具体、要靠前。避免"however, few studies have..."这类空泛。

- `Despite these advances, [specific unresolved problem] has received little attention.`
- `However, [existing approach] is limited by [concrete limitation], which restricts [consequence].`
- `The origin of [phenomenon] remains under debate, largely because [methodological reason].`
- `Whether [specific question] remains unclear.`

评述而非罗列——把前人工作收束到一个共同局限：

- `These strategies share a common limitation: [the shared drawback].`
- `While [approach A] improves [X], it does so at the expense of [Y].`

## Introduction：陈述本文目标与贡献

- `Here, we report [what], which [does what].`
- `In this work, we show that [key finding], enabling [consequence].`
- `We demonstrate that [X], and identify [mechanism/factor] as the origin of [effect].`

## Methods

一律过去时，被动或"we"主动都可，全文统一。

- `[Material] was synthesized by [method], following a modified procedure.`
- `[Measurement] was performed on [instrument] under [conditions].`
- `The [property] was determined from [data] using [model/equation].`
- `All potentials were referenced to [reference electrode] and corrected for iR drop.`

## Results：报告结果

图表展示什么用现在时，测得什么用过去时。给数值、给条件。

- `Figure 2a shows [what the figure shows].`
- `As shown in Figure 3, [observation].`
- `Under [conditions], [system] delivered a [metric] of [value].`
- `The [property] increased from [A] to [B] as [variable] was varied from [x] to [y].`

## Results：比较与强调

Results 的第一要义：不给光秃秃的数字，给基准和倍数/百分点。

- `This corresponds to a [N]-fold increase over [baseline].`
- `[Sample] outperformed [reference] by [Δ] under identical conditions.`
- `The [metric] ([value]) is among the highest reported for [class of materials] (Table S1).`
- `Notably（少用；确需强调时）, [the unexpected/most important observation].`

数值对比表达（把形容词换成数字）：

- `superior activity` → `a [N]-fold higher [rate]`
- `much lower overpotential` → `an overpotential [Δ mV] lower than [reference]`
- `excellent stability` → `retained [N]% of its initial [metric] after [time/cycles]`

## Discussion：归因与机理

因果强度与证据等级匹配（见下节 hedging）。避免用结论解释结论。

- `The enhanced [property] can be attributed to [mechanism], as evidenced by [data].`
- `[Observation] indicates that [interpretation].`
- `Combined with [technique A] and [technique B], these results suggest that [mechanism].`
- `This is consistent with [theory/literature], which predicts [expectation].`

排除竞争性解释（诚实处理，不 overclaim）：

- `Although [alternative explanation] cannot be fully excluded, [evidence] points to [preferred explanation] as the dominant factor.`

## Discussion：hedging 分档

按证据等级选词——这是全文最容易失衡、最招审稿人意见的地方：

| 证据等级 | 用词 | 例 |
|---|---|---|
| 直接证据 | demonstrate, show, confirm | `TEM directly shows the core–shell structure.` |
| 间接/推断 | suggest, indicate, imply | `The blue shift suggests quantum confinement.` |
| 推测 | may, likely, could, presumably | `This may arise from surface reconstruction.` |

- 降 overclaim：`X is the main reason for Y` → `X is a major contributor to Y`。
- 升不当的软弱：有直接证据却写 `may indicate` 是自我贬低，改成 `shows`。

## Conclusion

拔高到意义，不复述数字；展望要诚实、不许愿。

- `In summary, we have shown that [key finding], which [significance].`
- `These results establish [material/strategy] as [role], and provide a general strategy for [broader goal].`
- `[Remaining challenge] will need to be addressed before [application].`（诚实的局限，胜过 pave-the-way 式空许诺）
